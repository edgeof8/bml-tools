llm-prep

Any article, ready to paste into an AI. One click from any webpage.

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar. Visit any article, then click to copy an AI-ready prompt.

🤖 llm-prep

Or click to copy the bookmarklet code, then create a new bookmark and paste it as the URL.

How it works

1
Open any article — news, research papers, blog posts, documentation.
2
Click llm-prep — extracts the main content, strips ads and clutter, wraps in a prompt.
3
Paste into Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI — fully formatted prompt is already in your clipboard.

Output example

Here is an article titled "The Future of Solar Energy". Please summarize the core arguments in 5 bullet points.

Article:

# The Future of Solar Energy

solar will be the cheapest source of electricity in most countries...
