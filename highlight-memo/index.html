highlight-memo

Collect multiple highlights as you read. One click to export them all as a Markdown list.

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar. Highlight text and click to capture. Click with no selection to export.

🖍️ highlight-memo

Or click to copy the bookmarklet code, then create a new bookmark and paste it as the URL.

How it works

1
Highlight any interesting passage and click the bookmarklet to "queue" it.
2
Repeat as you read the article. highlight-memo remembers them all.
3
Click again (with nothing highlighted) to copy all captures to your clipboard.
